# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want this project to be a respectful and productive place for maintainers,
contributors, and users. Participants are expected to communicate with empathy,
assume good intent when reasonable, and focus discussions on the project.

## Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Respect different experience levels and viewpoints.
- Keep feedback constructive and specific.
- Accept maintainers' decisions after a discussion has reached a conclusion.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or imagery.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Repeated disruption of project discussions.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments, issues, pull requests, or other
contributions that violate this code of conduct. Repeated or severe violations
may result in temporary or permanent restrictions from participating in the
project.
